课程介绍


       本课程目标为，掌握线性代数的基本理论与方法，培养解决实际问题的能力，并为学习相关课程及进一步扩大数学知识面奠定必要的数学基础。
       教学内容为行列式、矩阵的定义和计算，线性方程组的解法，线性空间和线性变换概念和基本运算，欧几里得空间和n元二次型的概念和方法。
       线性代数是讨论代数学中线性关系经典理论的课程，它具有较强的抽象性与逻辑性，是物理及其应用学科的一门重要的基础理论课。由于线性问题广泛存在于物理科学及其应用技术的各个领域中，而某些非线性问题在一定条件下，可以转化为线性问题，因此本课程所介绍的方法广泛地应用于物理及其应用各个学科。

The goal of this course is to master the basic theory and method of linear algebra, to cultivate the ability for solving practical problems, and to lay the necessary mathematical foundation for the study of relevant courses and further expanding knowledge.
Teaching contents include the definition and calculation of determinants, matrices, solution of linear equations, linear space and linear transform and basic operation, Euclidean space and Nary real quadratic.
Linear algebra is the classical curriculum to discuss linear relationship in algebra. It has a strong abstraction and logic, is an important basic theoretic course in physics and applied disciplines. Due to the linear problem exists widely in all areas of physical science and technology, and some nonlinear problems in certain conditions can be transformed into linear problems, so this course introduces a widely method for the application in physics and other disciplines.
