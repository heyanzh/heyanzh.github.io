教学大纲


第一章 行列式(4学时)
重点：行列式的计算
难点：为行列式展开和拉普拉斯定理
要求：
(1) 理解二阶、三阶和n阶行列式的定义，并利用行列式定义能计算一些特殊行列式的值
(2) 掌握行列式的主要性质，利用行列式的性质化简和计算行列式
(3) 掌握行列式余子式和代数余子式的定义，能用拉普拉斯定理计算高阶行列式的值

第二章 矩阵代数(6学时)
重点：矩阵的代数运算和求逆
难点：初等变换和求逆矩阵
要求：
(1) 理解矩阵的概念和各种矩阵的定义,如方阵,复矩阵同型矩阵等
(2) 熟练掌握矩阵的代数运算法则, 如矩阵的加法与矩阵的数乘, 矩阵之间的乘法，并能灵活应用
(3) 理解逆矩阵和伴随矩阵的概念,并用伴随矩阵求逆矩阵
(4) 掌握用克莱姆法则求解线性方程组
(5) 掌握矩阵初等变换定义, 用初等变换求解逆矩阵
(6) 理解转置矩阵的定义及运算规律
(7) 掌握几种重要的方阵的性质,如对称矩阵,正交矩阵,厄米特矩阵和酉矩阵等
(8) 掌握分块矩阵的定义及运算特点

第三章: 线性方程组(5学时)
重点：解线性方程组和解的结构
难点：向量组和矩阵的秩定义和求秩
要求：
(1) 理解线性相关与线性无关的概念,掌握向量组线性相关的充要条件
(2) 理解极大线性无关组的定义和向量组的秩的概念, 掌握求解向量组秩的方法
(3) 理解矩阵秩的定义和求解矩阵秩的方法
(4) 掌握齐次线性方程组和非齐次线性方程组的求解方法
(5) 掌握齐次和非齐次线性方程组的解的结构

第四章: 线性空间(5学时)
重点：线性空间基底和坐标
难点：基底和坐标之间的变换关系
要求：
(1) 理解线性空间和子空间的概念及它们之间的关系,掌握线性空间的基本性质
(2 )理解n维线性空间的定义, 掌握在n维线性空间中一个向量与n个基底之间的关系,在基底下坐标的定义
(3) 掌握在n维线性空间中基底变换的概念和方法
(4) 掌握在线性空间中坐标变换的方法

第五章: 线性变换(6学时)
重点：线性变换和基底之间变换，求矩阵的特征根和特征向量
难点：基底之间变换矩阵
要求：
(1) 理解线性变换的概念, 掌握线性空间中线性变换的性质及线性变换下象子空间的定义
(2) 掌握线性空间在取一个基底下之后向量及线性变换后的矩阵表示方法
(3) 掌握线性变换在不同基底下矩阵之间的关系
(4) 理解矩阵的特征根和特征向量的概念
(5) 掌握矩阵的对角化方法求矩阵的特征根和特征向量

第六章 欧几里得空间(5学时)
重点：欧氏空间定义和标准正交基
难点：求欧氏空间的标准正交基
要求：
(1) 理解向量的内积和欧几里得空间的概念，掌握欧几里得空间内积的基本性质
(2) 理解欧几里得空间中的长度的概念
(3) 掌握欧几里得空间中两个任意向量线性相关的充要条件
(4) 理解在n维欧氏空间中标准正交基底的概念
(5) 掌握在n维欧氏空间中用施密特方法求出标准正交基底
(6) 理解正交变换的定义及正交变换的充要条件
(7) 理解正交矩阵的定义，正交变换和正交矩阵的关系

第七章 n元实二次型(5学时)
重点和难点：用正交变换化二次型为标准形方法
要求：
(1) 理解n元实二次型的定义及合同矩阵的定义和性质
(2) 理解二次型的标准形，掌握用配平方法求二次型的标准形方法
(3) 理解惯性定理，用对角矩阵方法求二次型的标准形及相应的坐标变换
(4) 掌握二次型的正定或负定分类方法
(5) 掌握用正交变换化二次型为标准形方法

Chapter 1 Determinant (4 hours)
Key: Calculation of the determinant
Difficulty: Determinant expansion and Laplace theorem
Requirements:
(1) to understand the definition of second order, third order and nth-order determinant, and to calculate the value of some special determinant by using determinant definition.
(2) master the main properties of the determinant, using them to make the determinant simple, then calculate the determinant.
(3) to master the definition of complement and algebraic complement, be able to calculate the value of higher order determinant by Laplace theorem.

Chapter 2 Matrix Algebra (6 hours)
Key: Algebraic operation and the inverse of the matri
x Difficulty: Elementary transformation and the inverse
Requirements:
(1) understand the concept of the matrix and the definition of various matrix, such as square matrix, complex matrix in the same type matrix, etc..
(2) be skilled in the algebraic operation law, such as the matrix addition, the matrix multiplierand matrix multiplication.
(3) understand the concept of the inverse matrix and adjoint matrix, and use the adjoint matrix to find the inverse matrix.
(4) master the Cramer’s rule for solving linear equations.
(5) master the definition of elementary transformation of the matrix, using elementary transformation to solve the inverse matrix.
(6) grasp the definition and operation rule of the transposed matrix.
(7) master the properties of several kinds of important matrices, such as symmetric matrix, orthogonal matrix, Hermite matrix and unitary matrix, etc.
(8) master the definition and operation characteristics of the partition matrix.

Chapter 3 Linear Equations (5 hours)
Key: Solving the linear equations and know the structure of the solutions
Difficulty: Vector group and the rank of the matri
x Requirements:
(1) to understand the concept of linear correlation and linear independence, to grasp the necessary and sufficient condition of linear correlation of the vector group.
(2) to understand the concept of the definition of maximal linearly independent group and the rank of vector group, and to grasp the method of solving the rank of vector group.
(3) to understand the definition of matrix rank and the method of solving matrix rank.
(4) the solution of the homogeneous linear equations and the non-homogeneous linear equations.
(5) the structure of the solution of homogeneous and non-homogeneous linear equations

Chapter 4 Linear Space (5 hours)
Key: Basis of linear space and coordinates
Difficulty: Transformation relationship between the base and the coordinates
Requirements:
(1) to understand the concept of linear space and subspace and their relations, to grasp the basic properties of linear space.
(2) to understand how the n-dimensional linear space is defined, and grasp the relationship between a vector with n substrates in n-dimensional linear space, the definition of the coordinates in the basement.
(3) master the concepts and methods of the base transformation in n-dimensional linear space.
(4) master the method of the coordinate transformation in the linear space.

Chapter 5 Linear Transformation (6 hours)
Key: Transformation between the linear transformation and the base, to obtain the characteristic root and characteristic vector of the matri
x Difficulty: Base transformation between matrices.
Requirements:
(1) to understand the concept of linear transformation, to grasp the nature of linear transformation in the linear space and the definition of the image subspace of the linear transformation.
(2) to master the matrix representation of linear space after taking a vector and using linear transformation.
(3) master the relationship between linear transformations in different matrices.
(4) know the concept of the characteristic roots and characteristic vectors of the matrix.
(5) grasp the diagonalizing methods of the matrix to get the characteristic roots and vectors.

Chapter 6 Euclidean Space (5 hours)
Key: Definition of Euclidean space and the standard orthogonal basis, Standard orthogonal basis for Euclidean space is the topic.
Requirements:
(1) understand the concept of vector product and the Euclidean space, grasp the basic properties of the inner product of Euclidean space.
(2) understand the concept of Euclidean space in length.
(3) Master the necessary and sufficient conditions for two arbitrary vectors’ linear correlation in Euclidean space.
(4) understand the concept of standard orthogonal basis in n-dimensional Euclidean space.
(5) master the Schmidt method to get standard orthogonal basis in n-dimensional Euclidean space.
(6) understand the necessary and sufficient conditions for the definition of the orthogonal transformation and the orthogonal transformation.
(7) understand the definition of orthogonal matrix, orthogonal transformation and orthogonal matrix.

Chapter 7 N-ary Real Quadratic Form (5 hours)
Key and Difficulty: Quadratic Form of the orthogonal transformation.
Requirements:
(1) understand the definition of N-ary real quadratic form and the contract matrix.
(2) understand the standard form of the Quadratic form, grasp the standard method for balancing the quadratic form.
(3) understand the inertia theorem, using the diagonal matrix method to find the quadratic form of the standard form and the corresponding coordinate transformation.
(4) grasp the positive definite or negative definite classification method.
(5) master the method of orthogonal transformation to standardize the quadratic form .


环节安排：
本课程共20周（每周2节，其中授课占18周，复习和期末考试占2周）。教学过程中，利用多媒体设备，提高学生积极性的同时，建立学生良好的物理图像。每周布置约八道习题，培养学生解决物理问题的能力，巩固所学的内容。教学内容之进度和安排，会根据实际教学情况，作适当小范围的调整。
There are 20 weeks for this course (2 class hours in a week, 18 weeks for in-classroom instruction, 2 weeks for revision and final examination). Through classes, we will take the advantage of multimedia to demonstrate the contents of courses. Not only can it raise students’ enthusiastic, but also is good for building up physics pictures. We will arrange about 8 questions every week, to cultivate their physical problem-solving ability and consolidate their knowledge. Besides, the schedule and arrangement of the courses will be slightly adjust according to the practical teaching situation.

教学方法：
课堂讲授为主，隔几周安排一次习题讲解。
In-classroom instruction is the principal teaching methodology; there will be an explanation of the exercises every several weeks.
