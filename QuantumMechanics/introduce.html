课程介绍


       本课程讲述量子力学的基本原理、基本方法、典型问题和应用，包括微观体系的波粒二象性、波函数的统计解释、力学量的算符表示、对易关系及其期望值、态的运动方程、态和力学量的表示理论、电子自旋和多粒子的全同性原理和波函数的交换对称性等基本概念，典型问题（量子阱、线性谐振子、中心场和氢原子、以及电磁场中）的量子态的求解、微扰法、量子跃迁和量子散射的基本问题和计算方法。本课程是为物理、光学信息与工程、材料科学等专业开设的，学生应具备经典物理学的基础知识、微积分、线性代数、概率统计、常微分方程和偏微分方程的基础知识。

This course presents the basic concepts and fundamental principles of quantum mechanics, and some typical issues and their relevant methods of quantum mechanics. The topics cover the particle-wave duality of the microscopic systems, the statistical interpretation of the wave function, the operator representation and the commutative relation of the physical variables and their expectation values, the Schrodinger equation, the representation theory, the electron spin and the exchange symmetry of the identical particles, the quantum well, harmonic oscillator, central force and hydrogen atom problems, the particle in electromagnetic field, the perturbation theory, the quantum transition and the scattering problem. This course is for the students who major physics, optics and materials. The students should have the basic background in classical physics, calculus, linear algebra, probability and statistics, differential equation and partial differential equation.
