课程介绍


目标与任务：使学生对宏观机械运动的规律有全面系统的认识，对理论力学中基本概念和原理有清晰的物理图像，掌握处理力学问题的一般方法，为后续课程理论物理课程的学习打下坚实的基础。培养运用数学工具解决物理问题的能力。
内容覆盖面：牛顿力学中的牛顿力学基本原理、有心运动与两体问题、非惯性参考系、质点组动力学，分析力学中的约束、自由度和广义坐标、虚功原理、拉格朗日方程组、微振动、哈密顿函数和正则方程、哈密顿原理和正则变换。
课程特点：注重讲授理论力学中的概念、原理和物理意义，并配以直观的例子来阐释。

Objective and task: To provide students with the overall knowledge of macroscopic mechanical movement, clear physical picture of the basic concepts and principles contained in theoretical mechanics, general method to deal with mechanical problem, and solid foundation in studying follow theoretical physics courses. To develop students' abilities to solve physics problems by using mathematical tools.
Content: Basic principles in Newton mechanics, central force motion and two-body problem, non-inertial reference frame, dynamics of system of particles, constraints, degrees of freedom, generalized coordinates, principle of virtual work, Lagrange's equations, micro-vibration, Hamiltonian function and canonical equation, Hamilton principle and canonical transformation.
Features: To focus on the teaching of physical concepts, principles and meaning, and together with visual examples.
