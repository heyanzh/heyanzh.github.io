课程介绍


本课程是理论物理四大力学中的一门，目的是研究热现象和规律，包括从宏观唯象出发的热力学理论和从微观的出发的统计物理理论。希望通过本课程的学习，掌握平衡热力学和统计物理的基本概念、规律、处理问题的方法和所含物理图像，牢固掌握热力学和近独立粒子系统统计，基本掌握系综理论，对热力学和统计物理的新进展有一定的了解。

Thermodynamics and statistical physics study thermal phenomena of macroscopic objects and statistical laws from microscopic view. Thermodynamics is their phenomenological theory and statistical physics is their microscopic theory. This is a basic course through which one can understand the thermal behaviors of the objects around oneself from their constituents. We also study the fundamental knowledge and skills of equilibrium thermodynamics and statistical physics in the course.
