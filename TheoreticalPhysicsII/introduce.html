课程介绍


       理论物理II包括量子力学、热力学与统计物理两大部分。首先讲述量子力学的基本原理、基本方法、典型问题和应用，包括微观体系的波粒二象性、波函数的统计解释、力学量的算符表示、对易关系及其期望值、态的运动方程、电子自旋和多粒子的全同性原理和波函数的交换对称性等基本概念，典型问题（量子阱、线性谐振子、中心场和氢原子、以及电磁场中）的量子态的求解。学生应具备经典物理学的基础知识、微积分、线性代数、概率统计、常微分方程和偏微分方程的基础知识。然后介绍统计物理的基础知识，如粒子的微观态描述，不同体系的微观态计算; 主要介绍玻尔兹曼统计，包括玻尔兹曼统计的推导，热力学量的统计表示，经典近似，理想气体的热力学函数，固体热容量等。之后介绍量子统计，如玻色-爱因斯坦分布和费米-狄拉克分布。本课程还包含系综理论。

Theoretical physics II has two parts: quantum mechanics, thermodynamics and statistical physics. In the first part, we introduce the basic concepts and fundamental principles of quantum mechanics, and some typical issues and their relevant methods of quantum mechanics. The topics cover the particle-wave duality of the microscopic systems, the statistical interpretation of the wave function, the operator representation and the commutative relation of the physical variables and their expectation values, the Schrodinger equation, the electron spin and the exchange symmetry of the identical particles, the quantum well, harmonic oscillator, central force and hydrogen atom problems, the particle in electromagnetic field. The students should have the basic background in classical physics, calculus, linear algebra, probability and statistics, differential equation and partial differential equation. In the second part, we talk about the thermodynamics and statistical physics. We first introduce the laws of thermodynamics, thermodynamic potentials. Then we focus on the basic knowledge of statistical physics including the microstates, entropy, ensemble theory, Boltzmann statistics and thermodynamic functions, classical approximation, thermodynamic functions of ideal gas, heat capacity of solids, etc. Then we study quantum statistics, which includes the Bose-Einstein distribution and Fermi-Dirac distribution. This course also includes the ensemble theory.
