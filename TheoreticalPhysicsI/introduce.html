课程介绍


       理论物理I包括理论力学、热力学与统计物理两大部分。首先从运动学为基础展开，介绍质点运动学，刚体运动学和转动坐标系，重点是质点运动学，它们是学习分析力学的基础，然后简要介绍牛顿力学的传统内容，包括牛顿方程，运动定理，特别是质心运动定理。分析力学主要介绍虚功原理，拉格朗日方程及其应用，小振动，哈密顿正则方程等内容，重点是拉格朗日方程及其应用。热力学与统计物理部分首先简单介绍热力学的基本理论，重点是一些热力学函数及其相互关系。然后介绍统计物理的基础知识，如粒子的微观态描述，不同体系的微观态计算; 主要介绍玻尔兹曼统计，包括玻尔兹曼统计的推导，热力学量的统计表示，经典近似，理想气体的热力学函数，固体热容量等。之后介绍量子统计，如玻色-爱因斯坦分布和费米-狄拉克分布。本课程还包含系综理论。

Theoretical physics I has two parts: theoretical mechanics, thermodynamics and statistical physics. In the first part, we introduce particle kinematics, rigid body kinematics and rotating coordinate system, with an emphasis on particle kinematics because it is prerequisite for learning analytical mechanics. Then we briefly introduce traditional Newtonian mechanics, including Newton’s equation, theorem of motion, especially theorem of motion of mass center. Analytical mechanics includes virtual work principle, Lagrange equation and its application, small vibration, Hamilton canonical equation, with an emphasis on Lagrange equation and its application. In the second part, we talk about the thermodynamics and statistical physics. We first introduce the laws of thermodynamics, thermodynamic potentials. Then we focus on the basic knowledge of statistical physics including the microstates, entropy, ensemble theory, Boltzmann statistics and thermodynamic functions, classical approximation, thermodynamic functions of ideal gas, heat capacity of solids, etc. Then we study quantum statistics, which includes the Bose-Einstein distribution and Fermi-Dirac distribution. This course also includes the ensemble theory.
