课程介绍


       课程目的是建立与狭义相对论一致的电荷和电磁场的经典概念，学习经典电磁相互作用的普遍规律、典型电磁现象及其描写方法。具体要求如下：
       要求牢固掌握 (1)真空和介质中的麦克斯韦方程组及其边值关系、洛伦兹力公式，和所涉及的矢量代数和矢量场分析运算； (2)电磁场的能量密度、动量密度、能流密度和动量流密度；（3）线性各向同性电磁介质模型；(4) 理想金属模型；（5）静电场和静磁场问题的常用求解方法； (6)电磁波传播和辐射的典型范例；(7) 狭义相对论和四维电磁势。
       一般掌握 (1) 超导体的唯象理论； (2)电磁场中带电粒子的拉格朗日量和哈密顿量； (3)高速带电粒子与电磁场相互作用的经典理论；（4）带电粒子的电磁场对粒子本身的反作用。
       一般了解磁矢势的量子效应、等离子体、光子晶体、光学空间孤子、光陷阱、辐射频谱等现象。

The objectives of the course are to establish the fundamental concepts of charge and electromagnetic (EM) field that are consistent with the theory of special relativity, to learn the general laws of classical EM interaction, typical EM phenomena and their description.
Basic requirements of the course are including: (1) mastering Maxwell’s equations and their boundary relations, the Lorentz force law, and vector algebra and vector field analysis in the context; (2) the energy density and flux, the momentum density and flux of EM field; (3) the linear isotropic models for dielectrics and paramagnetics; (4) the model for ideal metal; (5) common methods for solving the electrostatic and magnetostatic problems; (6) typical phenomena of EM field propagation and radiation; (7) the theory of special relativity and the four dimensional vector potential of EM field.
The students should be familiar with: (1) the phenomenological theory for superconductivity; (2) The classical Lagrangian and Hamiltonian of charged particles in EM field; (3) the classical theory for the interaction of high speed charged particle and EM field; (4) the reaction of the EM field of a charged particle on the particle itself.
The students should also know some advanced topics such as the quantum effect of the magnetic vector potential, plasma, photonic crystal, the optical spatial soliton, optical trap, and radiation spectrum.
